About Intelpod

Bringing ancient wellness wisdom to modern homes

Our Story

Intelpod was founded with a vision to make traditional Ayurvedic steam therapy accessible to everyone. We blend ancient wellness practices with modern technology to create premium steam bath cabinets that bring the spa experience to your home.

Our flagship product, the SvedaPod Classic, is designed with precision and care, using high-quality FRP materials and advanced steam technology. Each unit is manufactured in India, supporting local craftsmanship and the Make in India initiative.

Quality First

We never compromise on quality. Every product goes through rigorous testing to ensure it meets our high standards.

Customer Focused

Your satisfaction is our priority. We're committed to providing exceptional service and support.

Innovation

We continuously innovate to bring you the best wellness solutions with cutting-edge technology.

Our Vision

Establish global leadership in wellness by bridging Ayurveda and technology to make holistic health accessible & effective worldwide.

Our Mission

Deliver revolutionary wellness through blending advanced technology with Ayurveda, providing safe, effective, and accessible holistic health solutions globally.
